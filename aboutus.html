About Us

Astrid Non-Profit addresses and discusses feminism in society, shedding light on issues such as inequality in women's rights, the culturally constructed role of women, stereotypes and more. Our main initiative is to educate people on feminism and help young girls grow into strong women. We mainly provide basic information on feminist topics on our Instagram and host community events to raise funds. Founded in 2020, our new 2023-launched team is taking off again, more determined and passionate. Join us as we embark on our journey towards equality and ending discrimination!
